Section 01

Executive Summary

AuraFlow is building a compounding AI operating system for small businesses. Every client gets a dedicated AI agent that learns their specific business — their leads, their patterns, their team, their language. Over time, that agent becomes the institutional memory of the business.

The critical insight is that data compounds. A generic AI assistant forgets every conversation. AuraFlow agents remember everything and get smarter with every interaction — not just for that client, but by contributing to an anonymized intelligence pool that makes every other client's agent smarter too.

◆

The 3-sentence pitch: AuraFlow gives every small business owner an AI advisor that knows their business as well as they do. It learns from every conversation, every lead, and every outcome — and improves automatically. The longer a client stays, the smarter their agent gets, and the harder it becomes for any competitor to replicate what we've built.

This document outlines the 5-phase technical roadmap to build this system — from individual client agents (Phase 1, in progress) to an industry benchmark engine that no competitor can replicate without years of real client data (Phase 5).

Section 02

The Problem We're Solving

Business owners have data but can't act on it

A home service business owner has leads in Angi, ad campaigns in Google, reviews on Google Business, and a CRM with 200 contacts. None of these tools talk to each other, and none of them tell the owner what to do next. They're drowning in data and starving for decisions.

Current AI tools start fresh every conversation

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — every conversation begins with zero context. The business owner has to re-explain their situation every time. There's no learning, no accumulation, no compounding value. It's a calculator, not an advisor.

Generic advice vs client-specific intelligence

Generic AI tools give generic answers. "Respond to leads faster" is advice that applies to every business. What RC Generators & Electric actually needs to know is: "Your Angi lead response time jumped from 4 minutes to 22 minutes on Tuesday — that's why your leads dropped 60% this week. Specifically."

The forgetting problem is a trust problem

When an AI forgets what a client told it last week, the client stops trusting it. They stop using it. The value never compounds. AuraFlow's architecture solves this at the infrastructure level — memory is not an afterthought, it's the foundation.

→

The market doesn't need another AI chatbot. It needs an AI that knows your business, remembers your history, and gets smarter the longer you use it.

Three Memory Layers

AuraFlow agents operate across three distinct memory layers that compound over time:

LayerWhat it storesWhere it livesLifespan
Layer 1 — Preferences Extracted client preferences: communication style, priorities, concerns Supabase chat_context Permanent
Layer 2 — Conversation History Full session transcripts, cross-session recall via search Hermes SQLite (Railway) Permanent
Layer 3 — Skills Business-specific playbooks written by the agent itself Hermes Skills directory Versioned, improves over time

How a Conversation Flows

Client types message in the app ↓ /api/chat/route.ts ↓ 1. Auth check (Supabase) 2. Fetch 30-day metrics, leads, activity 3. Fetch Layer 1 memory (chat_context preferences) ↓ POST to Hermes on Railway Header: X-Hermes-Session-Id = client_id ↓ Hermes resumes that client's session Loads Layer 2 (conversation history) Loads Layer 3 (client-specific skills) ↓ Agent loop — up to 90 reasoning turns Uses tools if needed (search, code, APIs) ↓ Streams response back through the app ↓ Saves to Supabase · Extracts new preferences ↓ Client sees response in real time
Section 04

The 5 Phases

Phase 01
Individual Client Agents
Each client gets their own dedicated Hermes AI agent running on Railway, connected to their Supabase data and learning from every conversation.
  • Dedicated Hermes Docker instance per client on Railway (~$15-20/mo)
  • Persistent memory keyed by client_id session — never forgets
  • Connected to client's real business data: leads, metrics, activity
  • Autonomous reasoning loops up to 90 turns per response
  • Self-writes skills from repeated patterns over time
  • Multi-channel: WhatsApp, Telegram, or app — same agent
● In Progress — April 2026
Phase 02
Anonymous Telemetry Pipeline
Client agents report anonymized performance patterns upward to a central AuraFlow database — building the intelligence foundation without exposing any client's private data.
  • Nightly n8n job strips PII and aggregates patterns per industry
  • New aggregate_insights table in master Supabase project
  • What flows up: close rates, response time correlations, lead source performance, seasonal patterns
  • What stays private: names, contacts, financials, communications
  • Privacy boundary enforced at the data pipeline level, not policy
◎ Next — May 2026
Phase 03
AuraFlow Master Brain
A dedicated internal Hermes instance trained on all aggregate patterns — the intelligence engine that no individual client agent can become alone.
  • Not client-facing — internal AuraFlow intelligence only
  • Generates real industry benchmarks from actual multi-client data
  • Powers Foundation Score with live weights instead of static assumptions
  • Identifies what separates top-performing clients from struggling ones
  • Requires minimum 10 active clients for statistically meaningful patterns
◌ Planned — June 2026
Phase 04
Skill Distribution System
The Master Brain writes new skills based on what works across all clients and distributes them down to every client agent automatically — the entire network gets smarter at once.
  • Master Brain identifies high-value playbooks from aggregate patterns
  • Writes versioned skill files: lead-followup-sequence-v2.md
  • Skill registry with version control and rollback capability
  • Client agents pull new skills on next conversation start
  • Example: "3-touch follow-up in 72hrs increases close rate 40%" → pushed to all home service agents
◌ Planned — July 2026
Phase 05
The Benchmark Product
The intelligence becomes a client-facing product feature — giving every client a view of how they compare to anonymous peers in their industry. This is the moat made visible.
  • New dashboard section: "How do I compare?"
  • Benchmarks: close rate vs industry median, response time vs top performers, ROAS vs similar spend
  • Actionable gap analysis: "You close 28% vs industry average 41% — here's why"
  • Becomes a standalone sales tool: show prospects what top performers look like
  • Requires 10+ clients per industry vertical for statistical validity
◌ Planned — Q3 2026
Section 05

The Competitive Moat

Why this can't be replicated quickly

Any competitor can build a chat interface in 2 weeks. They cannot replicate 2 years of real client data, learned skills, and industry patterns. The moat is not the software — it's the accumulated intelligence that only exists because real clients used the system over time.

The Network Effect

Every new client makes every other client's agent smarter. Their anonymized patterns contribute to the Master Brain, which pushes better skills to all agents. A 50-client AuraFlow is fundamentally more intelligent than a 5-client AuraFlow.

The Switching Cost

A client who leaves AuraFlow after 12 months loses their agent's entire memory — 12 months of learned preferences, conversation history, and business-specific skills. Starting over with any other tool means starting from zero.

The Data Advantage

Industry benchmarks built from real small business data don't exist anywhere. Angi, Google, and CRM vendors have transaction data. AuraFlow has reasoning data — what decisions led to what outcomes for which business profiles.

The Skill Library

By Phase 4, AuraFlow has a library of proven, real-world playbooks for home services, construction, law, healthcare, and every other vertical we serve. These are written by AI from actual results — not hypotheses from consultants.

The compounding curve

10 clients → Basic patterns, early skill development 50 clients → Industry-specific playbooks, reliable benchmarks 200 clients → AuraFlow knows more about SMB lead conversion than any consultant alive 500 clients → The data moat is unbreakable. Years to replicate. Value per client compounds ───────────────────────────────────→ Month 1 Month 3 Month 6 Month 12 Month 24 Generic Familiar Expert Indispens. Irreplaceable
✓

The business that can't be replicated isn't built with better code. It's built with more time, more real data, and more compounded learning. AuraFlow's architecture is designed to accumulate all three.
